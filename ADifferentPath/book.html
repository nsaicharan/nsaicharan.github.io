A Different Path. Using the teachings of Ghandi and Dr King in a practical

can accomplish real systemic change.

The goals are clear:
* Build a bridge of trust back to our own government.
* Correct all the problems associated with our current economic system.

We can move our society to one currently based in fear and competition
to one of love and appreciation for each other.
