The method of petition is the ticket to real change.
The petition gives us a place to unite and to work from
the same page. It is a vehicle for our voice.

We are asking you to join us and let us explore the possibilities
of changing our society to one based in
appreciation and collaboration.

Your petition is state based as we are asking for our
individual states to call for an Article V Convention.

This Convention is to be convened with the the sole purpose of
drafting a bill that containing 3 Constitutional Amendments
that we the people will vote on to ratify.

  1. Abolishment of the electoral college
  2. A replacement of the House of Representatives with
    the well connected people of the United states.
  3. Acceptance of the contribution system of economics
