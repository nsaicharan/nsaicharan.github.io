Federal Reserve

From the economic panic of 1907, the Federal Reserve was created on a secret train to Jekyll Island in 1910 by a handful of very rich bankers headed up J.P. Morgan.

In 1908 political power met head on with big money when Nelson Aldrich called upon bankers to craft the Federal Reserve Act.

7 years after signing The Federal Reserve Act President Woodrow Wilson expresses his regret of handing over the nation to a system of credit controlled by a few dominant men.

The truth is the Federal Reserve is a privately held bank cartel that prints money out of nothing and then lends it to our country making money on the interest of this money.
