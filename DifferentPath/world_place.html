World Peace

One king elected by the United Nations.

We have heard the saying, “be the change you want to see in the world.”
But the fact that I have a savings account, doesn’t mean our government
will be free of debt. Or because I am wealthy doesn’t end the fact that
1 in 7 people are hungry.

The issues we face are systemic, not mild legislative, budget reform.
What we are talking about in A Different Path are systemic changes,
where greed no longer becomes necessary, where collaborating
makes much more sense than competing and where
we look to apply our works as creative gifts from the heart,
not only where they are needed but where they will be appreciated.

If we can accomplish a true systemic change to our economy
and a fundamental shift in the way we serve each other,
our next step will be one world king.

Using the United Nations where each nation becomes a collective
and committed to it’s unification and authority for greater humanity,
we can elect one person as the head of states
thus providing the catalyst for unification.

One step at a time, we can overcome greed and power struggles.
We can embrace unique cultures and communities.
We can learn a different way of thinking in a new society
where love and support for each other prevails.

This all begins with You and your signature to support change.
