Being Appreciated

In 1943 Abraham Maslow wrote a paper titled The Theory of Human Motivation which quickly became accepted as a cornerstone of modern psychology.

It’s theory is simple that if your survival requirements are met, appreciation and respect that you receive from others will help you achieve growth to a self-actualization or your greatest self.

The change we are talking about fosters an appreciation for one another and gives a value to everyone’s contribution. All while providing a baseline of survival and security for humanity as a whole.

We can create a change to a society that fosters a creativity and acceptance, giving everyone a much greater chance at their greatest self.

Join us in creating a change that brings out the inner gifts in all of us.
