Where is the society of our world heading?

We can all agree that as a country we are on a path with an economic system that is both unsustainable and actually works against our ability to live in harmony with our planet. In addition few of us doubt that we have come to a place where we believe big money is controlling our government.

We have been in a war over oil for to long, the chasm of inequality continues to grow straining our ability to feed 870,000,000 people that go hungry (more than the United States and the European Union).

Continuing on our current path is we all share in a growing fear of the society we are creating for ourselves, our children and the generation for the next 100 years.

It is time we change the direction of our current path.
