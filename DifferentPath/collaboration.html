Collaboration

We as a people have been asking for change for a long time. It is almost required to be part of your campaign slogan if you want to be elected. But the truth is the change we seek is a systemic change.

The change we speak of involves a fundamental shift to how we serve each other, from competing with each other to collaborating with each other.

The question is, should we really argue about who can move the pile of sand more efficiently and then there be one winner and many losers who are left to look for other piles?

Without question we are forced with our current economic system to serve the dollar more than we serve each other.

Within the proposed contribution system, we can work together allowing the ability to serve each other much easier with a systemic change more aligned with who we are.

Help us, together we can make and be the change. Please support our petition and learn more about what we are trying to accomplish.
