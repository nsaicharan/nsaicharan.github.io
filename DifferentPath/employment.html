Full employment seems like a foreign concept with our current economic system.

The third amendment proposed within A Different Path outlines acceptance the contribution system. This is an alternative to our current economic system. It opens the door for each of us to give in the way we can. To grow in the direction of our choice.

A change to the Contribution System runs by simple appreciation for one another. Giving thanks much like we give feedback to an online seller. The gratitude documented with our Contribution Card and system of feedback for our services is the basis for which we ask others for goods and services.

This opens the door to whatever your contribution may be, whether you create beautiful wood music stands, grow corn, teach, construction, it’s our community it is as good as the sum of it’s parts.

Moving our society from one of competition to that of appreciation and trust for one another is what we are asking your support and acceptance of change.
