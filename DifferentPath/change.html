A Different Path outlines a way in which we the people
can truly change our society.

We start by petitioning our states to call for an Article V –
Constitutional Convention. We are clear about the
amendments we wish to be on the drafted bill.
Clarity if where we begin our united campaign as
we the people of the United States..

Our goals:
  • 1 – Build a bridge of trust back to our own government
  • 2 – To correct all of the problems associated with our
    current economic system
Our amendments:
  • 1) Abolishment of the electoral college
  • 2) A replacement of the House of Representatives with
    the well connected people of the United states.
  • 3) Acceptance of the contribution system of economics

The solution includes full employment and some fundamental
shifts in our thinking that opens the door to all possibilities.
