Breif Introduction

Sonarika Bhadoria, popularly known as Parvathi, is a professional actress and model based in India.

She made her debut as an actress in a TV show called 'Tum Dena Saath Mera' in 2011. After that, she got the opportunity to act as 'Parvati' in 'Devon ke Dev.. Mahadev', which brought her immense fame and popularity.

Right now she is acting in various south Indian movies.
